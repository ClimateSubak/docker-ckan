What is the Subak Data Catalogue?

A collection of climate and energy data resources, starting with data created and curated by our Member Organisations, the datasets they use in their work, and expanding to a wider range of datasets into the future. We believe curating datasets that are well documented and clearly connected can accelerate our ecosystem - our Data Cooperative community.

Who is this for?

Anyone who makes use of climate data. Our first users are the analysts, researchers and engineers at non-profit organisations fighting climate change. We know there are a multitude of different people who use climate data for a huge range of different tasks and we hope to serve a range of different communities.

How can I add my data?

Create an account and let us know at datacooperative@subak.org what data you would like to share. We can then make you an admin for an organisation. You'll be able to create datasets for that organisation. Any submission will need to be moderated before it goes live on the website. Any issues, get in touch.
